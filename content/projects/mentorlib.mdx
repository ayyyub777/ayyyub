# Project Overview

Mentorlib is an innovative platform designed to connect entrepreneurs with industry experts for insightful reviews of business deliverables, such as pitch decks, marketing strategies, and business plans. The platform aims to streamline the process of seeking expert advice, making it accessible and efficient for startups and entrepreneurs.

## Key Features

### Onboarding with Role Selection

Users can register as either a client or an expert, allowing them to set up their accounts based on their roles. This tailored onboarding process ensures that each user has access to the features and functionalities relevant to their needs.

![Onboarding](http://localhost:3000/projects/mentorlib/onboarding-feature-min.jpg)

### Browsing Experts and Services

The platform provides a user-friendly interface for browsing through a diverse range of experts across various fields. Users can filter experts based on their expertise, reviews, and availability, making it easy to find the right fit for their needs.
![Browsing](http://localhost:3000/projects/mentorlib/browsing-feature-min.jpg)

### Availability Management

Experts can manage their availability through an integrated system, allowing them to set specific times for consultations and reviews. This feature ensures that users can easily find and book appointments that fit their schedules.
![Availability](http://localhost:3000/projects/mentorlib/availability-feature-min.jpg)

### Appointment Scheduling

Once users find an expert that meets their needs, they can seamlessly book appointments through a streamlined scheduling interface. Users receive confirmations and reminders for their appointments, enhancing the overall experience.
![Appointment booking](http://localhost:3000/projects/mentorlib/appointment-booking-feature-min.jpg)
